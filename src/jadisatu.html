Hi, I'm Ina Devina

Beginner Content Creator and Graphic Designer

I create impactful visual content that helps brands and events tell their stories in a powerful and engaging way.

ABOUT ME

"For me, creativity has always been a way to express what words sometimes cannot. I find beauty in small details. The warmth of light in a photograph, the energy of an event, the emotion hidden behind a design. Through content creation and graphic design, I try to turn those moments into visuals that feel alive. "I don't just want my work to be seen. I want it to be felt."

MY PROJECTS

UC Library

Content Creation (Internship)

As a content creator intern for UC Library, I contributed to developing creative and informative digital content. My role involved conceptualizing, designing, and producing visuals that made information more appealing and accessible to students.

UC Family Business

Content Editing (Internship)

During my internship, I was responsible for editing video content for UC Family Business on YouTube and Instagram. I transformed raw footage into engaging and informative content, ensuring each video aligned with the brand's message and visual identity.

Canva Design Projects

Personal & Ready-to-Use Templates

I actively create design projects using Canva for personal use and ready-to-use templates. I design layouts that are practical, visually balanced, and easy to customize for various needs such as social media posts, presentations, and promotional materials.

My Hobbies and Interest
